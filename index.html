Our process

01.Introduction

We start with an online meeting to discuss the strategy and plan of visual communication. Then we ask about client's history, who they are, what they want to achieve, in order for better understanding of the audience. The final step will be to set up the best direction.

02.Idea

Together we discuss vision and details. Following that, solid research is made to fit into client's needs related to the industry they operate in. The more we know, the better we are! At this point, we focus on the content presented on the website.

03.Design

That’s a moment when we share creative elements to achieve visual sense. Here we work on first sketches, share ideas to build wireframes and finally deliver the best design.

04.Development

At the time of development, we move Figma design to Webflow. Makeing accessibility for all devices from desktop to mobile is one of the main steps. Then we implement our SEO strategy to your website and show you how the website looks and works.

05.Onboarding

This is the phase where we may teach you and your team how CMS works, if you would be interested in that. Moreover, all access for marketing and analytics tools will be provided to your hands.

06.Website lunch

Here we go, now we are at the end of our journey. Your website is published on your domain and starts to bring you new customers.

It will depend on the scope of the project. During our first meeting, we can discuss every deadline and agree on a date for the website lunch. In general, a single landing page starts from 2-6 weeks, whereas a full website with 3-5 pages may take 6-8 weeks from design to publishing the website.
